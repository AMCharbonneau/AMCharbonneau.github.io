Clinical Experience

My clinical expertise is primarily within the medical home model, integrating psychological services into medical settings including primary care and pain management practices.

My training and background has included evaluation, assessment, and treatment treatment of people who have cancer, chronic pain, mood disorders, and substance abuse problems.

For a full CV and list of work experiences, please contact me directly.
